My Story

I’m a UX/UI product designer with a passion for creating experiences that balance user needs with business goals. Having owned a business, I understand both sides and excel at designing empathetic, user-centered solutions that also drive success for companies. Outside of design, I’m an adrenaline-seeker who loves traveling, trying new foods, and exploring new experiences like ATV riding, zip lining, and scuba diving. I’m excited to bring my unique blend of design skills, business acumen, and adventurous spirit to a role that inspires growth and connection.
